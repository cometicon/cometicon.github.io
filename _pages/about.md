<h2><b>Nicolas Comte - </b>
Computational Biology & Computational Imaging</h2>

Welcome to my personal page!

<i class="fas fa-user"></i>
My work focuses on reconstructing living forms through computational tools, bridging the gap between scientific precision and visual exploration.

<i class="fas fa-briefcase"></i> Professionally, I am a research engineer specializing in surface reconstruction and digital twins. I hold a PhD in Computer Science and an engineering degree in Computational Biology.

<i class="fas fa-camera"></i> In my creative practice, I explore extreme macrophotography. By applying computational reconstruction techniques, I aim to unveil the intricate, hidden geometry of the microscopic world.
